The Timber Buying Process

We know it can be overwhelming but we're here to help. We are happy to answer any questions you may have throughout the process.

1. Locate the Property

Once we locate your property lines we willsend one of ourtrained staff to appraise yourtimber.

2. The Offer

Once we have appraised yourtimber. We will make you an offer. Depending on the payment method you choose you can be paid upfront and we'll write the check tomorrow or you can be paid afterthe job hasfinished.

3. The Contract

Once you decide on payment we will have you sign a timber contract. This contract allows usthe rightsto cut the timber on yourland within 2 years.

4. The Cut

One of ourstaff members willset up one of our highly talented and experienced log crews on your property. They will explain where to cut and where to not, to your exactrequests. We love the land here at Southern Pines, if you have conservation goalsin mind, let us know and we're happy to help.

5. Follow Up

Throughout the harvest process do not hesitate to call usfor updates or any questions you may have. We will follow-up once the job endsto your satisfaction with this process.
