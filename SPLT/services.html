Clearcut

A method of regenerating a forest where all or most mature timber is harvested from a stand with a management plan in place to regenerate. This creates an even-aged stand with natural or artificially regenerated trees to the landowners specifications. Fun fact, cleared land creates peak wildlife environments in as little as one year after a cut or thinning.
