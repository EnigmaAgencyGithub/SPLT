Sell Your Timber

At Southern Pines Land and Timber our team works hard to provide the best pricing possible for your timber.

OUR MISSION

Southern Pines Land & Timber is committed to providing landowners with the knowledge they need to make the most informed decision possible when selling their timber. We want to preserve your land’s dignity and expedite the process. Depending on your specific goals we will provide management plans for future stand resilience, wildlife management or to optimize your land’s monetary value.
